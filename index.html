About Me

My name is Panagiotis Drakontis and I am a software engineer. I have been working as a full stack web developer since March 2012.


I graduated from the Technological Educational Institute (TEI) of Thessaly, Larissa Greece with a BA in Computer Science and Telecommunications in March 2010.


Web development is one of my passions. I am a ruby enthusiast and I believe that a feature could not be considered completed without unit testing. I love to learn new patterns and new technologies and apply them to my work. I am also a big fan of the open source philosophy and I believe that it will be the future of computer science. As a student I was a co-founder of the open source community of the TEI of Thessaly.


Since March 2012 I have been working as a full stack web developer at E-travel, which is one of the biggest online travel agencies in Europe. In E-travel I was a member of the team that developed the company’s CRM/ERP system until January 2017. Since then I am a member of the team that is responsible the front-end part of the company’s site.


In addition, for the past two years I have been the CTO and co-founder of techcareerbooster, while being responsible for developing its platform. Techcareerbooster is an e-learning web platform offering programming courses, with the guidance of dedicated mentors.
